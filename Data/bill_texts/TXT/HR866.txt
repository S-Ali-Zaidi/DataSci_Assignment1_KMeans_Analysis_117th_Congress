IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

Mr. RODNEY DAVIS of Illinois submitted the following resolution; which was 
referred to the Committee on the Judiciary 
RESOLUTION 
Recognizing that allowing illegal immigrants the right to 
vote devalues the franchise and diminishes the voting 
power of United States citizens. 
Whereas voting is fundamental to a functioning democracy; 
Whereas the Constitution prohibits discrimination in voting 
based on race, sex, poll taxes, and age; 
Whereas it is of paramount importance that the United 
States maintains the legitimacy of its elections and pro-
tects them from interference, including interference from 
foreign threats and illegal voting; 
Whereas the city of San Francisco, California, is allowing 
non-citizens, including illegal immigrants, to register to 
vote in school board elections;
