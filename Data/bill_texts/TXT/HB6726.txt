H. R. 6726 
To amend title 5, United States Code, to provide that a Member of Congress 
convicted of certain felony offenses relating to sexual abuse shall not 
be eligible for retirement benefits based on that individual’s Member 
service, and for other purposes. 
IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES 
FEBRUARY 15, 2022 
Mr. CROW (for himself, Ms. STEFANIK, and Ms. SPEIER) introduced the fol-
lowing bill; which was referred to the Committee on House Administra-
tion, and in addition to the Committee on Oversight and Reform, for a 
period to be subsequently determined by the Speaker, in each case for 
consideration of such provisions as fall within the jurisdiction of the com-
mittee concerned 
A BILL
