RESOLUTION 
Honoring our Nation’s fallen law enforcement officers for 
National Police Week. 
Whereas police officers protect and serve our Nation’s com-
munities; 
Whereas many brave men and women, deserving of honor, 
and with the last full measure of devotion, gave their 
lives in service of the Thin Blue Line; 
Whereas these fallen heroes deserve the highest respect; 
Whereas, in 2021, 73 police officers were killed in the line 
of duty protecting their respective communities, including 
5 police officers from the State of Louisiana; 
Whereas National Police Week is from May 11 until May 17
