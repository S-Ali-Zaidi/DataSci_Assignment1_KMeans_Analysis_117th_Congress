RESOLUTION 
Commending the Florida Everblades hockey team for winning 
the 2022 East Coast Hockey League’s Patrick J. Kelly Cup. 
Whereas, on June 11, 2022, the Florida Everblades hockey 
team won the 2022 Kelly Cup by defeating the Toledo 
Walleye by a score of 4 to 2 at Hertz Arena in Estero, 
Florida; 
Whereas the Everblades finished the championship game 
holding their opponents scoreless for the final 2 periods; 
Whereas the victory by the Everblades in the championship 
game— 
(1) marked the second time in 10 years that the 
Everblades have claimed an East Coast Hockey League 
(ECHL) championship; and
