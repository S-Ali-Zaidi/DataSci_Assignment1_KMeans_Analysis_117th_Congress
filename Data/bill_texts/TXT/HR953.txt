RESOLUTION 
Honoring the first responders who acted swiftly on Sunday, 
January 16, 2022, to ensure the safety of the residents 
affected by tornadoes that struck southwest Florida. 
Whereas, on January 16, 2022, multiple tornadoes touched 
down in Lee and Collier Counties, resulting in 
$8,300,000 in property damage, leaving 15,000 people 
without power; 
Whereas winds reached upward of 118 miles per hour, 4 peo-
ple were injured, 28 homes were destroyed, and 68 homes 
were deemed unlivable; 
Whereas the Lee County Sheriff’s Office, Fire Department, 
and Emergency Medical Services acted swiftly to assess 
damage and account for the lives of all civilians affected 
by these tornadoes; and
