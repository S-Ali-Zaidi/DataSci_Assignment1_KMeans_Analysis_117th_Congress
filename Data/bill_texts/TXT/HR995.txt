Supporting the Ukrainian people and recognizing their 
contributions to the State of Ohio.

Belarus, launched an unprovoked wide-scale attack on 
the peaceful, independent country of Ukraine;

mitted war crimes and crimes against humanity;

nity and children’s hospital, killing and injuring innocent 
civilians, including children; 
Whereas hundreds of civilians have been killed, and thou-
sands have been injured as a result of Russian attacks;
