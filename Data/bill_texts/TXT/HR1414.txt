IN THE HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES

Ms. DEAN submitted the following resolution; which was referred to the 
Committee on House Administration 
RESOLUTION 
Expressing the sense of the House of Representatives that 
firearms should not be present at any Federal election site. 
Whereas, on most days in 2022, there has been a mass shoot-
ing in the United States; 
Whereas an average of more than 110 Americans are killed 
and more than 200 are wounded by guns every day, 
which is an average of more than 40,000 deaths per year; 
Whereas, as of September 27 of this year, 33,183 people were 
killed by guns in the United States this year; 
Whereas, in May, a shooter killed 19 children and 2 teachers 
and wounded at least 17 others at an elementary school 
in Uvalde, Texas, carrying out one of the deadliest mass 
shootings in United States history;
