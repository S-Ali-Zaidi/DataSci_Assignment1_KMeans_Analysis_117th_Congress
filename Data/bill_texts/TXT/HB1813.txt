A BILL 
To amend title 49, United States Code, to ensure that reve-
nues collected from passengers as aviation security fees 
are used to help finance the costs of aviation security 
screening by repealing a requirement that a portion of 
such fees be credited as offsetting receipts and deposited 
in the general fund of the Treasury.
