RESOLUTION 
Expressing support for the designation of September 2021 
as ‘‘Peace Month’’ and calling on Congress to take action 
to promote peace. 
Whereas the United Nations designated September 21 as the 
‘‘International Day of Peace’’, otherwise known as Peace 
Day; 
Whereas the United Nations General Assembly declared 
Peace Day as a day devoted to ‘‘commemorating and 
strengthening the ideals of peace, both within and among 
all nations and peoples’’; 
Whereas all member states of the United Nations, organiza-
tions, regional and nongovernmental organizations, and 
individuals are encouraged to celebrate and recognize 
global peace efforts on Peace Day;
