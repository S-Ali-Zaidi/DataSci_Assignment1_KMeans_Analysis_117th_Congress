RESOLUTION 
Expressing support for designation of March 3, 2021, as 
National Triple-Negative Breast Cancer Day. 
Whereas triple-negative breast cancer was recognized as a 
distinct type of cancer in 2006; 
Whereas triple-negative breast cancer disproportionately af-
fects young women, Black and Hispanic women, and 
those with BRCA gene mutations; 
Whereas triple-negative breast cancers are estrogen receptor- 
negative, progesterone receptor-negative, and HER2-neg-
ative, making them distinct from other breast cancers; 
Whereas there is no targeted therapy for triple-negative 
breast cancers; 
Whereas 10 to 20 percent of all breast cancer diagnoses are 
designated as triple-negative;
