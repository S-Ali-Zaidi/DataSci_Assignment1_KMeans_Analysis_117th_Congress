RESOLUTION 
Expressing support for the designation of October 12, 2022, 
as ‘‘National Loggers Day’’. 
Whereas the logging industry has served as an economic driv-
er and cultural tradition in the United States for cen-
turies; 
Whereas the logging industry creates rural jobs and provides 
revenue for local and State governments and National 
Forests; 
Whereas loggers provide renewable material for products used 
by people in the United States every day; 
Whereas loggers are the first link in the $300,000,000,000 
domestic forest products supply chain;
