RESOLUTION 
Commending and congratulating the Coffeyville Community 
College Red Ravens men’s basketball team on winning 
the 2021 National Junior College Athletic Association 
Division I men’s basketball championship. 
Whereas, on Saturday, April 24, 2021, the Coffeyville Com-
munity College Red Ravens men’s basketball team (re-
ferred to in this preamble as the ‘‘Red Ravens’’) defeated 
the Cowley County Community College Tigers by a score 
of 108 to 99 in the 2021 National Junior College Ath-
letic Association (referred to in this preamble as the 
‘‘NJCAA’’) Division I men’s basketball championship 
game; 
Whereas the 2021 national championship is the first for the 
Red Ravens since 1962;
